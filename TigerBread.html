Tiger Bread

Tiger bread, also known as Dutch crunch and under various brandnames, is a bread of Dutch origin that has a mottled crust.

The bread is generally made with a pattern baked onto the top made by painting rice paste onto the surface prior to baking.

In January 2012, Sainsbury's announced that it would market the product under the name "giraffe bread", after a three-year-old girl's parents wrote to the company to suggest it.
